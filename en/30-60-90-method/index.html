30-60-90 Method

A structured 3-phase approach for fast and sustainable results

Why the 30-60-90 Method?

This proven approach generates visible results from the first few weeks while building a solid and sustainable strategy. Each phase has clear and measurable objectives.

30

Phase 1: Foundations

Days 1-30

Objectives

  • → Deep understanding of your business, market, and competition
  • → Define SMART objectives and precise KPIs
  • → Set up tracking and analytics tools
  • → Identify and launch first quick wins

Deliverables

Complete digital audit, 6-month marketing strategy, detailed personas, tracking dashboard, first campaigns launched.

60

Phase 2: Acceleration

Days 31-60

Objectives

  • → Deploy multi-channel campaigns at full capacity
  • → Continuously optimize based on collected data
  • → Launch A/B tests on key elements
  • → Scale the best-performing actions

Deliverables

Active campaigns on 3-5 channels, optimization report, A/B test results, first conversions and qualified leads.

90

Phase 3: Optimization

Days 61-90

Objectives

  • → In-depth analysis of performance and ROI
  • → Refine strategy based on learnings
  • → Automate working processes
  • → Plan next steps and scaling strategy

Deliverables

Complete 90-day report with ROI, strategic recommendations, roadmap for next 6 months, automated and optimized processes.
