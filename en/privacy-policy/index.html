Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

CliCom only collects personal data that you voluntarily provide to us through our contact forms:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company name (optional)
  • Message and information about your project

2. Data Use

Your personal data is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your contact and quote requests
  • Provide you with our marketing services
  • Improve our website and services

3. Data Protection

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is stored securely and is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Cookies

Our site uses essential cookies for its operation. For more information, consult our cookie policy.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with Swiss data protection law (FADP), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectify your data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@clicom.ch

6. Changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.
