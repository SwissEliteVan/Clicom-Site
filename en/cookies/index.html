Cookie Policy

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer when you visit a website. It allows information about your browsing to be remembered.

Cookies used on this site

Our site only uses essential cookies necessary for the proper functioning of the site.

Essential Cookies

  • Language preferences
  • User session
  • Security and fraud prevention

These cookies do not require consent as they are strictly necessary for the site to function.

Cookies we do NOT use

This site does not use:

  • Advertising tracking cookies
  • Social media cookies
  • Profiling cookies
  • Third-party analytics cookies

Managing cookies

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the site's functionality.
